Box Model: tells us how every element on a webpage is treated as a rectangular box.

4 important concepts(inside to outside)
1.Content: text/ image/ video | width x height|
2.Padding: space b/w content & border | transparent but it takes place|
3.Border: Line around the padding & content
4.Margin:Line Space outside the border, b/w the current element & other elements .
